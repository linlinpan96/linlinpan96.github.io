I don't pretend to understand the geopolitics of the middle east, or the necessity of deterrance.

Today I mourn. I mourn civilians caught under a conflict they have no control over, and a world where peace feels fragile.

From creation to re-creation, I pray re-creation come quickly.

Suffering in life is inevitable. It's important to find something worth suffering for. Give love freely. We are only here for so long.
